Why DAX Matters?

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) allows you to create new information from data already in your model. It enables:

  • Dynamic Aggregations: Calculate KPIs that respond to slicers and filters.

  • Time Intelligence: Effortlessly compute YTD, YoY growth, and moving averages.

  • Relationship Navigation: Pull values from related tables without VLOOKUPs.
